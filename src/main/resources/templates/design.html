How it works

Helping neighbors

High risk to virus

If you are a person higher risk, meaning that you have above 60 or you have other high-risk to this virus, you need to stay inside your house. Because of this you will need somebody to get your groceries, pick up prescriptions, and need to know that you are not alone in this outbreak.

After you signup for free to our website, a volunteer from your neighbourhood will see that you need help and he will call in order to establish a connection.

I want help
Low risk to virus

If you are a person with lower risk, meaning that you have below 60 you can help elders or other high-risk neighbors in community who needs to maintain social distancing in this outbreak.

After you complete the process of registration, you will see high risk people who lives nearby wich they need your help to get groceries, pick up prescriptions, and checking in every couple of day's. In order to comunicate with them you will recive there number so you are able to establish a connection.

How to protect
yourself

Wash your hands

This is a basic yet highly effective method to get rid off any germs and stop spreading them further

Don't Touch Your Face

Touching your face allows the germs on your hands to get into your eyes,mouth and nose, infecting you

Avoid Sick People

Avoiding places where over 100 people gather minimizes the risk of catching the virus and getting sick
